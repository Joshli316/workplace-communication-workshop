10 句职场常用语10 Essential Work Phrases
Learn these by heart — use them every day背熟这十句 — 每天用得到
英语English 中文Chinese 使用场景When to use
"Good morning." 早上好。Good morning. 一进门就先说When you arrive
"Excuse me, do you have a minute?" 不好意思，您有空吗？Excuse me, do you have a minute? 打断之前Before interrupting
"Could you help me, please?" 您能帮我吗？Could you help me, please? 礼貌求助Polite request
"I'm sorry, I didn't catch that." 对不起，我没听清楚。I'm sorry, I didn't catch that. 没听清时Didn't hear them
"How do you say ___ in English?" ___ 用英语怎么说？How do you say ___ in English? 学新词Learn a new word
"Sure, no problem." 好的，没问题。Sure, no problem. 接受任务Accept a task
"I'll be right with you." 我马上就来。I'll be right with you. 让对方稍等Asking them to wait
"Thank you for letting me know." 谢谢您告诉我。Thank you for letting me know. 收到信息后After being informed
"Have a good day." 祝您一天愉快。Have a good day. 下班/告别Leaving / goodbye
"See you tomorrow." 明天见。See you tomorrow. 下班时End of shift

⚠️ 重要 — 加州法律保障⚠️ Important — CA Law Protections
加州法律给员工每年带薪病假至少 5 天（小公司也算）。雇主不能因生病解雇您。任何雇主——包括无证工人的雇主——都必须遵守。 CA law gives every worker at least 5 paid sick days/year (even at small employers). Your employer cannot fire you for using them. All employers must comply — including those of undocumented workers.
